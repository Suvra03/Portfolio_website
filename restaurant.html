01 Project Description

DYNAMIC RESTAURANT WEBSITE

The Gas Distribution System Web Application is a modern, user-friendly platform built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and PHP, representing the cutting edge of technology in the gas distribution industry. This system is designed to streamline and enhance the management of gas distribution networks, allowing users to log in, monitor gas supply, place orders, and track deliveries, thus significantly improving efficiency and customer satisfaction. This project brings a digital transformation to gas distribution, making it more accessible and convenient for both providers and consumers, revolutionizing the way gas services are delivered.

02 Objectives

Objective 1

To create a user-friendly online platform that allows consumers to book gas cylinders easily and efficiently.

Objective 2

To provide gas distributors with a centralized system for managing gas cylinder orders, deliveries, and inventory.

Objective 3

To improve transparency by enabling consumers to track the status of their gascylinder orders.

Objective 4

To reduce manual paperwork and streamline the overall gas booking and delivery process.

Objective 5

To enhance the overall user experience and customer satisfaction.

03 Features

- For Consumers- For Distributors

User Registration & Login

- Users can create accounts and log in securely.

Distributor Dashboard

- A centralized system for gas distributors to manage orders and inventory.

Gas Cylinder Booking

- Consumers can place gas cylinder orders online.

Order Management

- Accept, process, and assign orders to delivery personnel.

Order Tracking

- Users can track the status of their orders in real-time.

Inventory Management

- Keep track of available gas cylinders and refill stock.

Payment Integration

- Secure online payment options for order confirmation.

Route Optimization

- Optimize delivery routes for efficient gas cylinder distribution.

Notification System

- Automated email/SMS notifications for order confirmation and delivery updates.

Analytics and Reporting

- Generate reports on order history and inventory.

04 Modules

Consumer Module

Allows customers to request, track, and pay for gas services, view consumption history, and receive notifications.

Distributor Module

Empowers gas distributors to schedule deliveries, manage inventory, track distribution routes, and optimize supply chain logistics for efficient gas distribution.

Admin Module

Enables system administrators to manage users, monitor gas distribution, track faults, and generate reports for efficient operations.
